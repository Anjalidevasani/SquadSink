About World

The world is the planet Earth, home to over 8 billion people and diverse ecosystems.

Basic Facts about Earth

  • Shape: Earth is an oblate spheroid—slightly flattened at the poles and bulging at the equator.
  • Diameter: About 12,742 km (7,918 miles).
  • Surface area: About 510 million square kilometers (71% water(H2O), 29% land).
  • Age: Approximately 4.54 billion years.
  • Distance from the Sun: About 149.6 million kilometers (1 AU).

Structure of the Earth

Earth has four main layers:

  1. Crust: The outermost layer (solid rock).
  2. Mantle: Semi-solid rock layer beneath the crust.
  3. Outer Core: Liquid iron and nickel.
  4. Inner Core: Solid iron and nickel.
